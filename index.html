About Clean N'Shine

Leicester's premier mobile valeting service with a passion for perfection

Our Philosophy

At Clean N'Shine, we believe that every vehicle deserves to look its best, regardless of age or condition. Our founder Elton started this business out of a genuine passion for automotive care and a commitment to delivering exceptional results.

We combine traditional hand-detailing techniques with the latest products and technology to achieve showroom-quality finishes. Our team undergoes continuous training to stay at the forefront of valeting innovation.

Why Choose Us?

  • Certified Professionals: Fully trained and accredited detailing specialists
  • Premium Products: We only use top-tier, vehicle-safe cleaning products
  • Mobile Convenience: We come to you at home or work, saving you time
  • Eco-Friendly: Water-efficient methods and biodegradable products
  • Insurance Approved: Fully insured for your peace of mind
  • Satisfaction Guarantee: Not happy? We'll make it right

Our Commitment

We're committed to more than just clean cars - we're dedicated to building lasting relationships with our customers through:

  • Reliability: On-time arrivals and consistent quality
  • Transparency: Clear pricing with no hidden costs
  • Attention to Detail: We notice and address what others miss
  • Custom Solutions: Services tailored to your vehicle's needs
  • Community Focus: Supporting local businesses and charities
